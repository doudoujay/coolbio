Biowatch

Wrist veins as a biometric

Secure Your World

We provide a very secure and user-friendly wearable vein biometric solution.

  1. Use our app for enrolling (locally) your vein pattern and selecting applications and devices you want to connect to your BIOWATCH.

  2. Wear your BIOWATCH and activate it by matching your vein pattern with your enrolled reference. Only you can activate your BIOWATCH. Your vein pattern is unique.

  3. When needed your BIOWATCH transmits your PKI encrypted user code. Start opening doors with no keys and forget about using passwords.
